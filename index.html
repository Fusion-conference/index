CALL FOR PAPERS



ICACDS-2021 conference will host Keynote Lectures, Tutorials, Workshops and Oral Presentations on all aspects of Advanced Computing. The Conference is being organized specifically to help the Computer Industry derive benefits from the advances of next generation computer and computing technologies.

Researchers invited to speak, will present the latest developments and technical solutions in the areas of: Advanced Computing and Data Sciences.

ICACDS-2021 is soliciting original, previously unpublished and high quality research papers addressing research challenges and advances in the tracks mentioned below. The maximum allowable length of the paper is 12 pages (including figures and references) for regular research articles strictly in SPRINGER SINGLE COLUMN FORMAT. Articles will be Double-Blind Peer-Reviewed by three experts to decide its suitability for publication in the conference. Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors by email.
